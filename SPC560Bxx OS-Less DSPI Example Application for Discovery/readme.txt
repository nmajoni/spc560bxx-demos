This application shows how to use the SPI driver 1 to manage the spi
communication using the port pins PA[14] (pin 30 on the external connector X1)
as SCK, PA[13] (pin 31 on the external connector X2) as SOUT, PA[12] (pin 31 on
the external connector X1) as SIN and PA[15] (pin 29 on the external connector
X1) as CS0.
